        ### General Plant Breeding
        An overview of the fundamentals of plant breeding, including genetic resources, genetic variation, reproductive physiology, and selection theory, as well as various breeding methods such as hybridization, heterosis breeding, and mutation breeding.

        ### Advanced Plant Breeding
        Topics include polyploidy breeding, chromosome engineering, plant tissue culture, genetic manipulation, transgenic plants, molecular markers for genome analysis, quantitative trait genetics, breeding objectives, and seed management.
