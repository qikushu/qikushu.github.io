      text: |
        ### From Fukuoka Airport
        1. Subway Kuko Line: Fukuoka Airport Station → Meinohama Station (¥330)
        2. Transfer to JR Chikuhi Line: Meinohama → Kyudai-Gakkentoshi Station (¥230)
        3. Showa Bus: Kyudai-Gakkentoshi Station → Kyudai Kogakubu-mae (¥250)

        ### From Hakata Station
        **Option 1:** Subway + JR (same route as above from Meinohama)

        **Option 2:** Nishitetsu Bus from Hakata Ekimae A → Kogakubu-mae (¥670, approx. 1 hour)

        ### From Tenjin Station
        **Option 1:** Subway + JR (same route as above from Meinohama)

        **Option 2:** Nishitetsu Bus from Solaria Stage-mae → Kogakubu-mae (¥670, approx. 1 hour)

        ### By Car
        A campus entry fee of ¥300 is charged. Taxis may enter without charge.
